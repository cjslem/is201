The Slemboski Family About me

Born in St. George Ut. Jacob Slemboski is the 4th child in a family of 6 to parents Jimmy and Natalie Slemboski. Growing up he loved Mt. Bike, compete in pickle ball tournaments, play games and read. In High School he was the Senior Class President and worked at Costa Vida.

Currently Jacob is living in Provo as a freshman in the pre-business program at BYU. He plans to study Accounting and receive his Masters. He holds a job as a Book keeper in a local Solar company.
